Registration Information

Registration is open now at this website.
  • Full registration: $185 NZD
  • Student registration: $75 NZD
  • Full registration (early-bird): $140 NZD
  • Student registration (early-bird): $60 NZD

Deadlines

  • Early Bird deadline: 15 May, 2022 22 May, 2022.
  • Author registration deadline: 29 May, 2022. Presenters from all tracks (paper, demos, music, installations, doctoral consortium, worskhop conveners) must register by this date in order to have their work included in the conference program. Submissions without an appropriate registration will be withdrawn from the program and the proceedings.
